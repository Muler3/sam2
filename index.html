About Me

As an adaptable problem solver, over the years I have developed and produced events on a freelance and full time employment basis for some of the UK’s leading events companies – including The Pop Up Hotel, Boomtown Festival and Planit Events. Years of working in the events industry have given me an extremely diverse skill set ranging from promotion, to event planning, construction, production, rigging, health and safety and site builds. I’m a highly competent site manager, with excellent multi-tasking skills and qualifications. I have an array of construction and plant operation qualifications that make me ideal personnel to have on site, and I also have the knowledge of brand operations necessary to liaise with event partners, playing an indispensable role in the progress of large-scale, complex events.
